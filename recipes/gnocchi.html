homemade gnocchi

description

a simple, vegan gnocchi recipe to make gnocchi from scratch!

this recipe only requires three ingredients

ingredients

  • 2 pounds russet potatoes
  • 1 1/2 - 2 cups all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
  • 1 teaspoon salt

instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut into 1-inch chunks. Boil in salted water until very tender, about 15-20 minutes. Drain well.
  2. While the potatoes are still hot, pass them through a potato ricer or mash them very well with a potato masher until no lumps remain. Spread the riced potatoes out on a clean surface to cool for 10 minutes.
  3. Sprinkle 1 1/2 cups of the flour and the salt over the potatoes. Use your hands to gently knead the mixture until it comes together into a soft dough, adding a bit more flour if needed. Be careful not to overwork the dough.
  4. Divide the dough into 4 pieces. Roll each piece into a long rope about 3/4-inch thick. Cut the ropes into 1-inch pieces to form the gnocchi.
  5. If desired, roll each gnocchi over the tines of a fork to create ridges. Place the formed gnocchi on a floured baking sheet.
  6. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Working in batches, boil the gnocchi until they float to the surface, about 2-3 minutes. Remove with a slotted spoon.
  7. Serve the gnocchi immediately with your favorite sauce. Enjoy!
